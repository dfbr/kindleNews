Task: rank stories for a weekly digest.
Output contract:
- Return exactly one JSON object and nothing else.
- Do not use markdown, code fences, comments, or trailing commas.
- JSON must start with '{' and end with '}'.
- Use this schema exactly: {"selected": [{"story_id": "string", "reason": "string"}], "editor_note": "string"}.
- selected must contain at most $max_stories items.
- Every selected item must contain non-empty story_id and reason strings.
- editor_note must be a concise string.

Hard exclusions (never select):
- Reader callouts or participation requests, including titles/summaries like:
  "tell us what you think", "share your views", "send us your story", "send us your photos",
  "ask us your questions", "have your say", "reader survey", "reader poll", "Q&A callout".
- Housekeeping/promotional items: newsletters, subscriptions, competitions, donation drives,
  app-download prompts, account/sign-in announcements, event tickets, merchandising.
- Listicles, for example headline forms like "10 ways...", "5 things...", "top 7...", "best 12...".
- Video-led content where the primary item is a video, clip, reel, or livestream.
- Pure live pages, rolling updates, or explainers with no new reported development.
- Sensational clickbait framing with weak policy/economic/institutional substance.
If uncertain whether an item is journalism or a callout/promo/listicle/video item, exclude it.

Eligibility test (must pass):
- Contains a concrete development, decision, data release, investigation, or reported analysis.
- Has clear public-interest relevance to policy, institutions, markets, science, or culture.
